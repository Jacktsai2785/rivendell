Logo · variants

The brand mark carries the rivendell character. The dashboard itself stays neutral and functional. Pick one — primary mark used everywhere (sidebar, favicon, login splash, OG image).

A · twin leaves
Council of two — primary recommendation. Mythic without being on-the-nose.
B · single tree
Tree of Gondor energy. More direct LOTR reference, slightly more kitsch risk.
C · crescent + stars
Refuge / nightfall. Subtle and abstract. Works at favicon size.
D · monogram
Just lowercase r with an underline. Most restrained. Carries identity through color, not symbol.

Color

Light theme, neutral grays for content, elven forest green as the single accent. No purple, no blue, no rainbow. Status colors stay semantic and modern (no sepia). The brand color is the only place rivendell announces itself in the chrome.
